## ğŸ’¡ Best Practices

### For Descriptions:
- **Keep it short** but informative (1-2 sentences)
- **Mention key ingredients** (Nutella, pistachio, chocolate)
- **Use appetizing words**: Creamy, Rich, Moist, Fresh, Decadent
- **Highlight special features**: Handmade, Homemade, Premium, Authentic

### For Images:
- **Use high-quality photos** (not blurry)
- **Good lighting** (natural daylight is best)
- **Show the product clearly** (not too far away)
- **Nice background** (clean, simple)
- **Show texture** (layers, frosting, toppings)
